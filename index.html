04. Science

Academic & NLP Research

Bronze Award 2025

TAMZHI: Shorthand Romanized Tamil to Tamil Reverse Transliteration System

Published in: International Journal on Advances in ICT for Emerging Regions (ICTer), May 2024

TAMZHI solves the problem of typing Tamil characters on standard QWERTY keyboards by taking informal, shorthand Romanized Tamil (e.g. "vankam" or "nalam") and reverse-transliterating it back into correct Tamil script ("வணக்கம்").

Citation: Herath, A. D., & Sumanathilaka, D. K. (2024). TAMZHI: Shorthand Romanized Tamil to Tamil Reverse Transliteration Using Novel Hybrid Approach. International Journal on Advances in ICT for Emerging Regions (ICTer), 16(1).
